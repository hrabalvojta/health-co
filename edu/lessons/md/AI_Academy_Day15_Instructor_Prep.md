## SPRINT A: BUILD DECK (9:10–9:35, 25 min)

### Tool: Pitch Deck Generator (`pptx-generator.html`)

The generator is a 7-step wizard that produces a branded .pptx:

| Step | Slide | Content | Source |
|------|-------|---------|--------|
| 1 | Title | Name, role, date | Student fills in |
| 2 | What & Why | Problem + Hans finding + quantified benefit | Day 11-12 |
| 3 | How & Dependencies | Component + tech choice + dependencies | Day 13 |
| 4 | Governance | EU AI Act articles + obligation + gap | Day 14 |
| 5 | Success Metrics | 3 KPIs with targets and baselines | Day 11-12 |
| 6 | Risks & Mitigations | 3 risks with specific mitigations | Day 12, 14 |
| 7 | The Decision | Self-verdict + conditions + next steps | Student fills in |

### What to watch for:

| Situation | Intervention |
|-----------|-------------|
| Student stares at empty form | "Open your checkpoint posts from this week. Copy-paste into the fields." |
| Quantified benefit is vague ("improves efficiency") | "Give me the number. How many tickets? What percentage? How much money?" |
| Tech choice has no rationale | "Hans will ask 'Why this and not that?' Have your answer ready." |
| KPIs have no baselines | "You can't measure improvement without a starting point. Check Day 12." |
| Student finished early | "Open your deck. Read every slide as Hans would. What's the weakest point?" |
| Student polishing text at 9:30 | "Generate what you have. You can re-generate after Hans reveals your gaps." |

## SPRINT B: THE BOARDROOM (9:35–10:05, 30 min)

### Tool: Go/No-Go Simulator (`go-nogo-sim.html`)

**How it works technically:**
- Student opens The Boardroom page
- Deck data is automatically loaded from the generator (via localStorage)
- Hans Muller (AI) reads the deck content and asks role-specific questions
- 5-7 question rounds, each targeting a different aspect
- After final question, Hans delivers verdict: GO / GO WITH CONDITIONS / NO-GO
- Scores: Strengths (1-10), Risk Awareness (1-10), Readiness (1-10)

**What Hans asks about (based on deck content):**

| Topic | Example Question | What He's Testing |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| ROI / Numbers | "Walk me through that 30% figure" | Can they defend their math? |
| On-prem constraints | "Where's the GPU budget for Llama 3.1?" | Do they understand real constraints? |
| Compliance | "Stefan keeps emailing me about AI Act. Is your system high-risk?" | Do they know their governance obligations? |
| Change management | "Jan's team thinks they're being replaced" | Do they think about people, not just tech? |
| Dependencies | "What happens if AI-SEC's PII filter doesn't work?" | Do they have fallback plans? |
| Timeline | "The board meets in August. What's delivered by then?" | Is the timeline realistic? |

**Scales to 200 students simultaneously** — no peer matching, no logistics, no DM chasing.

### What to watch for:

| Situation | Intervention |
|-----------|-------------|
| Student doesn't have deck data loaded | "Go back to generator, fill in at least the key fields, then return to Boardroom." |
| Student gives one-word answers to Hans | "Hans is a CIO. He wants specifics. If he asks about 30%, give him the calculation." |
| Student gets No-Go and is discouraged | "Good. Hans told you exactly what's missing. Fix it this weekend. That's the point." |
| Student gets Go and stops | "Congratulations. Now look at your scores. Which is lowest? That's your Weekend fix." |
| API error / Hans not responding | Students can retry. If persistent, they should note their self-verdict and move to Sprint C. |
