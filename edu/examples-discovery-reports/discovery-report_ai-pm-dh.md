# Discovery Report – AI Program Manager  
**Client:** EuroHealth Insurance AG  
**Sponsor:** Hans Muller (CIO)  
**Program Type:** AI Governance Industrialization  
**Target Autonomy Level:** L2 (Constrained Agent)  
**Timeline:** 6 Months  
**Stated Budget:** €180K (Phase 1)

---

# 1. Executive Reframing

EuroHealth does not have an automation problem.  
It has an AI governance fragmentation problem.

Three departments operate AI independently (Moveworks, HR chatbot, Claims LangChain) without centralized policy, oversight, or auditability.

This program must deliver simultaneously:

1. Measurable cost-efficiency trajectory  
2. Centralized AI governance control  
3. EU AI Act readiness evidence  

Failure in any one dimension results in program failure.

# 3. Financial Model & ROI Stress Test

## Baseline (To Validate Week 1)

- Estimated annual helpdesk cost: ~€380K  
- Stated automation rate: 35% (unverified)  
- Target: Reduce remaining manual workload by 40%

Projected annual savings range:

| Scenario | Automation Achieved | Annual Savings |
|----------|--------------------|----------------|
| Conservative | 25% | €90K |
| Target | 40% | €120K |
| Optimistic | 50% | €150K |

---

## Infrastructure & Operating Cost Risks

- GPU CapEx (on-prem): €60K–120K  
- Ongoing OpEx (monitoring, compliance, updates): €40K–60K annually  

Worst-case break-even could extend to 3 years if cost-only framing is used.

---

## Strategic ROI Positioning

The financial case must combine:

- Cost reduction trajectory  
- Regulatory exposure reduction  
- AI sprawl containment  
- Enterprise scalability foundation  

Pure cost narrative is fragile.  
Governance + risk mitigation narrative is durable.

# 4. Autonomy Level Decision

**Target: L2 (Constrained Agent)**

- AI auto-resolves defined L1 categories  
- Humans monitor & override  
- Full audit logging  
- Escalation guardrails  

L1 is insufficient for cost impact.  
L3 is unacceptable under EU AI Act risk exposure.

---

# 5. Industrialization Defined

Phase 1 must deliver:

- Central LLM Gateway (single inference entry point)  
- Policy-as-Code enforcement layer  
- Model registry & versioning  
- Prompt version auditability
